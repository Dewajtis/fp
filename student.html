I`m 17 years old. My favorite subjects: mathematics, physical education, working with databases. I`m studying on second course in Ivano-Frankivsk National University of Oil and Gas.

IFNTUOG was established in 1967, the university has been known for preparing qualified and experienced specialists for the oil and gas industries. However, it has expanded to offer education in economics, management and law that is directly related to the needs of energy industry.

Structure of university:

  • Institute of Architecture, Construction and Energy
  • Institute of Natural Sciences and Tourism
  • Institute of Humanitarian Training and Public Administration
  • Institute of Economics and Management
  • Institute of Mechanical Engineering
  • Institute of Information Technologies
  • Institute of Oil and Gas Engineering
  • Department of Military Training

History of university:

  1. 1960 year: the Stanislavsky General Technical Faculty of the Lviv Polytechnic Institute began its work
  2. 1963 year: the faculty was reorganized into the Ivano-Frankivsk branch of the Lviv Polytechnic Institute
  3. 1967 year: Ivano-Frankivsk Institute of Oil and Gas was established on the basis of the Ivano-Frankivsk branch of the Lviv Polytechnic Institute
  4. 1993 year: the institute received level V accreditation
  5. 1994 year: Ivano-Frankivsk State Technical University of Oil and Gas was established on the basis of the Ivano-Frankivsk Institute of Oil and Gas
  6. 2001 year: the university received national status
